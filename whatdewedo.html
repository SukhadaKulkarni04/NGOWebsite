What We Do?

Y4D Foundation is a Youth Platform and working on it's mission of "Empowering the Underprivileged". Y4D Foundation core belief is that only empowered citizen can drive a positive change in the society. Y4D Through it's various initiatives and partnerships working on to improve situation of underprivileged sections and building a society better place for living of future generation.


Donate a meal for one child and join our fight against malnutrition. 7,00,000+ children die due to malnutrition. Personality Development. Career Counselling. Training for employment. Types: Non-academic resources, Building libraries, Career counselling.

Children are the rocks on which society's future will be built,they are our greatest asset as a nation and the leaders of tomorrow. Y4D believes Every child in the world is entitled to get good health, quality education and a clean environment to grow. Y4D engages with children from stages of infancy, childhood to adolescence through focused interventions on their Health, nutrition, education and socio-emotional development. Y4D enables well nurtured children with full opportunities for growth in a safe and protected environment, through its various initiatives in the following areas: i)Health and Nutrition
ii)Education
iii)Socio-Emotional and Life Skills development
Youth being the backbone of society, Y4D facilitates harnessing and nurturing of their infinite potential and energy for their development and prosperity thereby making them harbingers of positive social change.
